Automated Emails for Custom Gradebook

Mastery based grading schemes are becoming more common in education. There are a variety of ways to implement these, but a common thread is the idea to allow students multiple attempts or multiple ways of demonstrating their knowledge and learning. I implemented at UMD in an introductory probability course. The built in gradebook in our learning management system could not handle the mix of letters and point values so I had to make a custom gradebook and wrote a Google Apps Script to send automated grade update emails each week to all of my students. The gradebook itself used lots of text and logic spreadsheet functions. Click below to see see a walkthrough and sample of this.

I've co-authored a handful of publications on handicap tournaments. With wonderful applications in tournament scheduling, these graphs are visually beautiful as well.
